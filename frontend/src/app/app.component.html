Exercising is often just more fun if done with other people.

We want to address this with our app.

We want to provide people an easy solution to plan sport activities together and even meet new people to exercise with in new places.

They can look for places and activities near them and take part.

This way the get to know new people with similar sporting interests and might find their new sporting buddy.

The app can be used by everyone who is interested in sports.
